In the Gallery.

Anne's work has been exhibited in collaboration with the Nelson-Atkins Museum of Art and the Kansas City Society of Contemporary Photography — two of the most respected institutions in the region.

Exhibition 01

Postcards from the Middle

Nelson-Atkins Museum of Art  ·  Kansas City Society of Contemporary Photography

"Postcards from the Middle" — a documentary photography series exploring life, landscape, and identity across the American midwest.

Exhibition 02

Portrait of a City

Nelson-Atkins Museum of Art  ·  Kansas City Society of Contemporary Photography

"Portrait of a City" — an intimate look at Kansas City's soul, its people, places, and the quietly extraordinary moments that define urban life.

The Artist

Hello, I'm Anne.

Hi, I'm Anne — a Kansas City-based fine art photographer and artist with a passion for capturing beauty in the quiet moments, the open skies, and the everyday extraordinary. My work spans original digital art, fine art photography, and intimate portrait sessions.

Every piece I create is made with intention — to live beautifully in your home and tell a story worth keeping.
